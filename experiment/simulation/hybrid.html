Network Topology: Hybrid


We are building a hybrid of these two topologies: STAR + BUS.

Steps:
  1. To perform the simulation (choose one from each category):
    End devices: PC or Laptop
    Central devices: Hub or Switch

  2. Wire to match the diagram: (1,2,3) → device-1, device-1 ↔ device-2, (4,5) → device-2.
    3. Note: Click a device port and drag to another device port to connect
  3. Click Check to validate the hybrid topology.
