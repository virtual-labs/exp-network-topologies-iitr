Network Topology: Mesh


To perform the simulation, follow the below given steps:
1. Click on the PC/laptop (present at the bottom) to place the device on the working area.
2. Next click on the switch or hub.
3. Now create a communication link as shown in Fig. 1 (click on the image for a better view).
  3.1. Connect each of the end devices with the corresponding switch/hub turn by turn, e.g., PC1 with Hub1, PC2 with Hub2, and so on.
  3.2. Now connect each switch/hub with the remaining three switches/hubs in your mesh network.
NOTE: Click a device port and drag and drop it to another device port to connect.


Fig. 1 Reference image for mesh topology

4. Once the topology is constructed, click on the check button to verify if the constructed topology is built correctly or not.
